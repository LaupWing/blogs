# Hook

The hook is the title + opening line(s). If this doesn't land, nothing else matters.

## What makes a strong hook

- Creates curiosity or tension immediately
- Makes a specific promise ("you'll learn X") or challenges an assumption
- Speaks directly to the reader's situation
- Avoids vague openers like "In this blog post I will..."

## Formats that work

- **Contrarian** — "Everyone says X. They're wrong."
- **Specific result** — "I doubled my traffic in 3 days. Here's how."
- **Problem-first** — "You're wasting time on X without knowing it."
- **Story** — Drop the reader into a moment. No setup.

## What to avoid

- Generic titles that could apply to any blog
- Starting with background/context before the hook
- Burying the interesting thing on line 3

## Claude's role

Don't rewrite the hook unless it's genuinely weak. If the draft already has a strong opening, say so. If not, offer 2-3 alternatives and explain why.
