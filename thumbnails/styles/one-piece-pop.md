# Style: One Piece Pop Manga

Create a vibrant manga comic layout drawn in the exact style of Eiichiro Oda's One Piece — specifically resembling One Piece color spreads and chapter cover pages.

Art style — this is critical:
- Characters must look like they are drawn by Eiichiro Oda: elongated limbs, very distinctive exaggerated features, detailed ink line work with varied line weights, cross-hatching for shading and muscle definition
- NOT western cartoon style. NOT smooth rounded Pixar/Disney faces. NOT generic anime. Specifically Oda's One Piece style: sharp angular features, detailed noses, expressive mouths showing teeth, heavily inked outlines that vary in thickness
- Characters should feel like they belong in One Piece — big bold silhouettes, unique designs, wild hair colors and styles, expressive body language bursting out of the panel frame
- Facial expressions are extreme: wide gaping mouths, eyes popping out, shock lines radiating, veins popping — but rendered in Oda's detailed manga ink style, not simplified cartoon
- Heavy use of black ink cross-hatching for shadows and depth, especially on muscles and clothing folds

Visual style:
- Bright, fully saturated color palette: yellows, greens, purples, hot pinks, reds, blues — no dark or muted tones
- Backgrounds use colorful halftone dot patterns, starburst rays, polka dots, and diagonal stripe effects — exactly like One Piece color pages
- Bold, thick varied-weight black outlines on all elements
- Sound effect text in the center in large stylized katakana-inspired lettering (bold, red or black, slightly tilted, integrated into the art as part of the composition)
- Speech bubbles with thick black outlines and bold text, cloud-shaped thought bubbles, directional arrows
- Widescreen landscape format (16:9 ratio)

Panel layout:
- Panels cut at sharp diagonal angles radiating outward from a central explosion or impact point — exactly like the One Piece reference image
- 4-6 panels in a dynamic radial composition — NOT a square grid
- Central area dominated by a large sound effect explosion shape
- Characters in surrounding panels are partially cut off by panel borders, as if bursting through them
- Thick black diagonal panel borders creating maximum chaos and energy
